  \item \textbf{[10 points]} Let $\Sigma$ be the set of lower and
    uppercase letters in the Roman alphabet plus the set
    $\set{[,],\_,.}$ of special symbols.  Let a \emph{word} be a
    nonempty string of lower case letters in $\Sigma$, a
    \emph{capitalized word} be an uppercase letter in $\Sigma$ or an
    upper case letter in $\Sigma$ followed by a word, a
    \emph{parenthesized word} be a string of the form $[x]$ where $x$
    is a word or capitalized word, and a \emph{sentence} be a string
    of the form $x.$ or $x\_y.$ (note the occurrences of ``.'') where
    $x$ is a capitalized word, and $y$ is a nonempty string of words,
    capitalized words, and parenthesized words separated by the symbol
    ``\_''.  Write a regular expression that matches the set of
    sentences (as defined here).

  \bigskip

  \textcolor{blue}{\textbf{Yunbing Weng, wengy12, 03/17/2019}}

  $set_{of}sentences = ((CapWord)(\_(Word+CapWord+ParWord))^*.)^*$

  \bigskip
